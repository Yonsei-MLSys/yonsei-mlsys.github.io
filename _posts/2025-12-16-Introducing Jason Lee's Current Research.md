<h5>
    Jason Lee - CIPLab, AI, Yonsei University
</h5>

This presentation introduces the current research of Jaeseong Lee (Jason Lee).
First, he introduces the research history and current research trend of deep learning based image super-resolution task.
Next, he introduces the challenges of current image super-resolution models and his attention on specific topic, which is planned to submit at ECCV 2026.
Finally, he will be discussing about the future of image super-resolution models and the key blue prints of next-generation image super-resolution models.
